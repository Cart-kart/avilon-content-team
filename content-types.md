## Content Type Definitions

### 1. KNOWLEDGE
| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Purpose** | Educate audience — build trust and authority |
| **Goal** | Awareness, reach, saves & shares |
| **Tone** | Educational, professional, data-backed |
| **Length** | Long (400–800 words) |
| **CTA** | Soft — "ติดต่อเพื่อเรียนรู้เพิ่มเติม" |
| **Agent** | tech-writer |
| **Post Day** | Monday |

**What it should contain:**
- How the technology works
- Industry stats or research-backed data
- Use cases and who benefits
- No direct product push — let the knowledge sell

**Good for:**
- Building brand credibility
- Reaching new audiences (shareable)
- SEO-style awareness on Facebook

**Example topics:**
- "โดรนในคลังสินค้า ทำงานอย่างไร?"
- "Indoor Navigation คืออะไร และทำไมถึงสำคัญ"
- "5 เหตุผลที่คลังสินค้ายุคใหม่หันมาใช้ Automation"

---

### 2. SOFT SELL
| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Purpose** | Connect pain points to product solution |
| **Goal** | Engagement, leads, DM inquiries |
| **Tone** | Empathetic, solution-focused, warm |
| **Length** | Medium (200–400 words) |
| **CTA** | Medium — "พร้อมให้คำปรึกษา สาธิต Demo ได้ทันที" |
| **Agent** | tech-writer |
| **Post Day** | Wednesday |

**What it should contain:**
- Open with a relatable pain point or scenario
- Show how the problem affects business
- Introduce product as the solution (benefit-led)
- End with low-pressure CTA + contact

**Good for:**
- Nurturing warm audience
- Decision-maker engagement (ops director, CTO)
- Moving people from aware → interested

**Example topics:**
- "นับสต็อก 3 วัน กลายเป็น 3 ชั่วโมง"
- "พนักงานขึ้นที่สูงทุกวัน — มีวิธีที่ดีกว่าแล้วค่ะ"
- "Inventory Error ทำให้ธุรกิจเสียหายเท่าไหร่กันแน่?"

---

### 3. TRENDJACKING
| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Purpose** | Ride current trends to boost reach and relevance |
| **Goal** | Reach, new audience, shares |
| **Tone** | Timely, insightful, forward-thinking |
| **Length** | Medium (200–400 words) |
| **CTA** | Soft to medium — tie trend back to product |
| **Agent** | tech-writer + trend-monitor |
| **Post Day** | Friday |

**What it should contain:**
- Reference a real current event, trend, or news
- Explain why it's relevant to warehouse/logistics/drone
- Connect to avilonROBOTICS product or expertise
- Position brand as thought leader

**Good for:**
- Expanding reach beyond existing followers
- Showing brand is current and relevant
- High shareability when trend is hot

**Example topics:**
- งาน Warehouse & Logistics Asia 2026
- กฎระเบียบ CAAT ใหม่เกี่ยวกับโดรน
- E-commerce growth → warehouse demand surge
- AI + Automation ในอุตสาหกรรม Logistics ไทย

**Rule:** Must tie to a REAL current trend — trend-monitor agent verifies this first.

### 4. HARD SELL
| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Purpose** | Direct conversion — get leads and demo bookings |
| **Goal** | Clicks, DMs, phone calls, demo requests |
| **Tone** | Direct, confident, benefit-driven |
| **Length** | Short-medium (150–300 words) |
| **CTA** | Strong — "นัด Demo วันนี้ ฟรี" + phone + email |
| **Agent** | ad-writer (3 variants) |
| **Post Day** | Sunday |

**What it should contain:**
- Lead with the #1 benefit (speed, accuracy, safety)
- Bullet list of key features/benefits
- Who it's for (target industries)
- Clear CTA with น้องฟ้าใส contact
- Urgency or offer if applicable

**Good for:**
- Retargeting warm audience
- End-of-week conversion push
- Campaigns with specific offers or deadlines

**Example topics:**
- "พร้อมเปลี่ยนการนับสต็อกให้เร็วขึ้น 4 เท่าไหมคะ?"
- "นัด Demo Photon Inventra ฟรี — ถึงที่"
- "ลดต้นทุน Cycle Count ได้ทันที — ติดต่อเราค่ะ"
